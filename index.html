ABOUT ME

I bring over six years of experience as a product analyst in the EdTech sector to my current journey into data science, complemented by my studies towards a second master’s degree in the field. In this AI+ era, I am driven by a passion for uncovering insights through data, combined with a solid foundation in learning and technical skills. My expertise in data analysis and a deep understanding of the education industry equip me to contribute meaningful insights that enhance decision-making across various sectors.

I am actively seeking PhD opportunities that align with my interest in data science and machine learning, particularly those aimed at creating social impact within education and healthcare. My goal is to leverage my capabilities to contribute to significant advancements in these critical areas, improving outcomes and fostering positive change.
